Scholarships and Financial Aid

Discover scholarships and financial aid opportunities available for Tanzanian students. Find out how to fund your education and achieve your academic aspirations.

Scholarship Name Open to Amount Details
Science Merit Scholarship University of Tanzania Computer Science All undergraduate students, Full tuition, Awarded based on academic merit. Renewable annually based on GPA.

Engineering Excellence Scholarship Engineering Students 75% of tuition Awarded to students demonstrating academic excellence in their high school results.
